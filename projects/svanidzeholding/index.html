About Us

Château Svanidze is a winemaking estate owned by the Svanidze family. It has a magnificent location at the heart of Kakheti region, near Signagi overlooking the Caucasus Mountains range. The design of the estate combines the delights of traditional Georgian architecture and rural landscapes with modern dynamic treatment of space and form. With 100 hectares of vines, the estate has been producing high-quality Svanidze wines for more than 150 years. Vines are cultivated while paying constant attention to preserving the estate’s natural biodiversity. Kakheti is a perfect region for wine tourism: road has various stages taking in vineyards, nature, gastronomy, heritage, culture. For two generations, the Svanidze family has been helping to revive this exceptional heritage and cultivate the Svanidze spirit, blending a timeless way of life with authenticity.

Visit Château Svanidze and succumb to the estate’s charms as you journey through time thanks to its ancient heritage.
